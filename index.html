How to Update Your TomTom GPS in 2025

Updating your TomTom GPS is simple if you follow the correct steps:

Step 1: Go to the Navisolve update portal or TomTom’s official website.

Step 2: Download and install TomTom MyDrive Connect on your Windows or Mac computer.

Step 3: Connect your TomTom device to the computer using a USB cable.

Step 4: Launch MyDrive Connect. It will automatically detect your device and show available updates.

Step 5: Select “Update” and wait for the process to complete. Keep the device connected until installation finishes.

Tips for a Smooth Update Process

  • 🔌 Keep your device connected to a power source

  • 🔄 Do not disconnect the GPS until the update finishes
  • 📋 Backup important settings before updating

Troubleshooting Common TomTom Update Problems

  • ❌ Device not recognized: Try another USB cable or port, restart MyDrive Connect
  • ❌ Update freezes: Close the app, restart your computer, and try again
  • ❌ No updates available: Your device may already be updated, or your subscription may have expired
  • ❌ Slow downloads: Switch to a wired internet connection
