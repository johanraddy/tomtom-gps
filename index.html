Update with MyDrive Connect

Overview

Welcome to the official guide for updating your TomTom device with MyDrive Connect in 2025. This guide covers essential steps, new features, compatibility, and best practices—ensuring your navigation stays accurate, efficient, and up to date.

Whether you're using a TomTom GO, VIA, Rider, or Start series device, MyDrive Connect is your reliable tool for downloading the latest maps and software. Let’s dive in.

How to Update via MyDrive Connect

  1. Download and install MyDrive Connect from the official TomTom site.
  2. Launch the application on your Windows or Mac computer.
  3. Connect your TomTom device with a high-quality USB cable.
  4. Select your device in MyDrive Connect, then click “Update Device.”
  5. Allow updates to download and install completely—do not unplug the device midway.
  6. After completion, safely eject and restart your TomTom to apply updates.

Supported TomTom Models

MyDrive Connect supports most modern TomTom devices, including:

  • TomTom GO Series
  • TomTom VIA Series
  • TomTom Start Series
  • TomTom Rider (for motorcycles)
  • TomTom XL/NAV Series (select models)

Always visit the TomTom Support page to verify your device’s compatibility and status.

Best Practices for Smooth Updates

  • Use a reliable USB cable—original TomTom cables are best.
  • Ensure your computer has minimal background processes to prevent interruptions.
  • Keep your TomTom connected to power during heavy updates.
  • Back up favorites and personal settings before updating.
  • Plan updates every 3–6 months to stay current with the latest improvements.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Device not detected: Try reconnecting or using a different USB port or cable.
  • Update stalls: Restart MyDrive Connect or reboot your computer and retry.
  • No update found: Check your subscription status—map updates may require an active plan.
  • Performance issues: Clear the app cache or reinstall MyDrive Connect.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I update wirelessly?
A: Yes—certain GO models support Wi-Fi updates directly on the device (check your model’s manual).

Q: Will my favorites be lost?
A: No—favorites are preserved. Still, it’s best practice to back up your device before updating.

Q: My device is old—can I still update?
A: Some older models may have reached their end-of-life for updates. Verify on the TomTom support pages or consider upgrading to a newer device.
